Campus Life

Castle of Dreams

DPS Castle of Dreams is an inspiring and ultra modern pre-school of DPS Indore. With its supportive and learning environment, DPS Castle of Dreams provides the best of education with a multi level experience for your child.It is believed that the maximum development and growth of a child’s brain is made in the early years. Knowing that the early childhood experiences are critical to the emotional and intellectual development of your child, we at DPS Castle of Dreams pay special attention to provide an ideal learning environment.
